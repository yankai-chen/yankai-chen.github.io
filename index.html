Yankai Chen

陈焱凯

Postdoctoral Associate
Weill Cornell Medicine
Cornell University

I am now a postdoctoral associate at Cornell University, working on topics of NLP for EHR data in healthcare, mainly funded by Prof. Yunyu Xiao. I received my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science and Engineering from The Chinese University of Hong Kong in 2023, supervised by Prof. Irwin King. Prior to that, I completed my M.S. degree at The University of Hong Kong in 2018, working with Prof. Reynold Cheng, and received my B.S. degree from Nanjing University in 2016, mentored by Prof. Qizhi Liu.

My research interest is machine learning for Information Retrieval, Processing, and Generation. My recent topics include but are not limited to:

  • AI for health data science, e.g., multimodal EHR data mining & predictive models, social risk & health determinant discovery.
  • LLM-related problems, e.g., PEFT, zero/few-shot learning.
  • Search, advertising, and recommender systems.
  • Large-scale graph data mining and learning problems, e.g., community retrieval, GNNs, self-supervised graph learning .
  • AI4DB problems, e.g., ANN search and optimization for vector database, motif prediction.

Feel free to contact me if you are seeking any form of collaboration.
