Google Scholar - GitHub - LinkedIn - CV

I received my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science and Engineering from The Chinese University of Hong Kong in 2023, supervised by Professor Prof. Irwin King. Prior to that, I completed my M.S. degree at The Univeristy of Hong Kong in 2018, working with Prof. Reynold Cheng, and received my B.S. degree from Nanjing University in 2016, mentored by Prof. Qizhi Liu.

My research is data mining and machine learning for Information Retrieval, Processing, Generation, and Protection. My recent topics include but are not limited to:

  • Graph data mining and learning problems, e.g., GNNs, self-supervised graph learning.
  • Data generation problems, e.g., generative models, watermarking techniques.
  • AI4DB problems, e.g., ANN search and optimization for vector database, motif prediction.
  • Quantization methods for efficient IR processing, e.g., learning to hash, product quantization.
  • Recommender systems and enhancing techniques, e.g., knowledge graph supplementary.

Feel free to contact me if you are seeking any form of collaboration.
